Witcher 3

The Witcher 3 is an action role-playing game that was released in 2015. It is based on the popular book series of the same name by Andrzej Sapkowski. The game follows the story of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter, as he navigates a war-torn world filled with political intrigue and supernatural creatures.

    Positive aspects

  • Immersive world-building that draws players into the game's universe
  • Complex and well-developed characters
  • Engaging and challenging combat system
  • Multiple endings that are determined by player choices

    • Negative aspects

  • Large open world can make the game feel overwhelming at times
  • Some players may find the combat system too difficult or repetitive
  • Quest structure can be confusing or repetitive
  • Some players may find the game's mature themes and content off-putting
