Hades

Hades is an action-packed rogue-lite game that was published in 2020. The game is based on Greek mythology and features impressive music. In Hades, players take on the role of Zagreus, the son of Hades, as he tries to escape the underworld. Along the way, he battles various enemies and bosses, gains new abilities, and interacts with a cast of interesting characters. The game received critical acclaim for its fast-paced gameplay, stunning visuals, and engaging story.

    Positive aspects

  • Fast-paced gameplay
  • Stunning visuals
  • Engaging story
  • Impressive music
  • Variety of abilities and characters to interact with

    • Negative aspects

  • Difficulty may not appeal to all players
  • Permadeath mechanics can be frustrating for some players
  • Repetitive gameplay may become tedious over time
  • Some players may not enjoy the theme or setting of the game
